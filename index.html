About The Revolving Kitchen

The Revolving Kitchen (“TRK”) is a digital marketplace helping food entrepreneurs gain access to commercially certified kitchen spaces, that are necessary to produce commercially saleable foods, at convenient locations and competitive rental rates. TRK achieves this by leveraging existing restaurant kitchens with spare capacity (i.e. when business is closed and/or shared kitchen), in addition to traditional commercial kitchens, thus taking away the need for entrepreneurs to invest heavily in establishing their own kitchen spaces during their conception stage. TRK is like AirBnB for Kitchen rentals.
